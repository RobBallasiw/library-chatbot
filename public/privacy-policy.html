Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our Library Chatbot ("we", "our", or "the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with our chatbot through our website or Facebook Messenger.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Messages you send to the chatbot
  • Questions and inquiries about library services
  • Conversation history during your session

2.2 Information from Facebook

When you interact with us through Facebook Messenger, we may receive:

  • Your Facebook Page-Scoped ID (PSID) - a unique identifier
  • Your name and profile picture (for librarian staff only)
  • Messages you send to our Facebook Page

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Session IDs for conversation tracking
  • Timestamps of messages
  • Technical information necessary for the chatbot to function

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide library assistance and answer your questions
  • Connect you with human librarians when requested
  • Improve our chatbot service
  • Maintain conversation context during your session
  • Manage librarian access and permissions (staff only)

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Conversation data is stored temporarily in server memory
  • Session data is cleared when the server restarts
  • We do not permanently store personal conversations
  • Librarian access information is stored in configuration files

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With authorized library staff to provide assistance
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our library and users

6. Facebook Messenger Integration

When you use Facebook Messenger to interact with our chatbot:

  • Your messages are sent through Facebook's platform
  • Facebook's own privacy policy also applies
  • We only receive information necessary to respond to your inquiries
  • You can stop receiving messages by blocking our page on Facebook

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Access controls for librarian staff
  • Regular security updates

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of the service at any time
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for general library users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. Third-Party Services

Our chatbot uses:

  • Ollama AI for generating responses (runs locally, no data sent to third parties)
  • Facebook Messenger Platform (subject to Facebook's privacy policy)
